Retail & eCommerce

Streamline your order management process
vTalk.ai retail voice AI handles tasks such as collecting, modifying, terminating orders, recording returns, and adjusting shipping and billing details. It also actively engages customers by reminding them to place another order based on prior orders. This function is similar to the tasks of a real sales assistant.

Hi, I'd like to check the status of my order.

Intent: /order_status

Of course, I'd be happy to help. Please provide your order number and the email address associated with your account.

Sure, my order number is 12345 and the email is john@example.com.

Entity: order_number=”12345”, email=”john@example.com”
Action - API Call: get_order_status(order_number=”12345”, email=”john@example.com”) - status OK

Thank you. I've located your order. It was placed on January 1st and is currently in transit. The estimated delivery date is January 10th. Is there anything else I can assist you with?

Get valuable insights with voice-powered feedback and surveys
AI voice assistants can be used in retail to gather customer reviews and feedback. They quickly tell you how they feel about your service and whether they have a fantastic experience from time to time or not.

Hi, I'd like to leave feedback about my recent order. I just received it and there's a problem.

Intent: /report_problem

Hello! I'm sorry to hear that. How can I help you with your order today?

One of the items I received is damaged. I would like to return it.

Intent: /return_request
Entity: reason=”damage”
Action: Go to /claim_registration

No problem, I can assist you with that. Please provide me with your order number and a brief description of the issue with the item.

Intelligent, convenient, personalized product choices
AI-based virtual voice assistants can offer personalized product recommendations in eCommerce by analyzing a customer's purchase history, preferences, and demographics. The voice assistant can provide highly relevant recommendations tailored to the customer's needs and interests using natural language processing and machine learning algorithms.

Hi, I'm looking for a new phone. Can you help me find one that fits my needs?

Intent: /items_recommendations
Entity: item_type=”phone”
Action: Go to /phone_recommendations

Of course! Can you tell me more about what you're looking for in a phone? Do you have a budget in mind?

I'm looking for a phone with a good camera and a budget of $500.

Entity: budget=”500 USD”, requirements[“good camera”]

Based on your requirements, I recommend the iPhone 12 Pro or the Samsung Galaxy S21. Both of these phones have excellent cameras and are within your budget. Which one would you like to know more about?

Customer satisfaction tracking
vTalk.ai solution provides you with another channel for customer feedback. It can call your retail customers any time and ask them about their experience, in addition to taking a satisfaction survey after a call. When customers offer you their time, you have a better chance of understanding if they are satisfied and discovering what factors affected their opinion.

How your recent shopping experience was with us?

It was great. I was able to find everything I needed and the checkout process was fast and easy.

Action: Go to /positive_feedback”

That's great to hear! On a scale of 1-10, how would you rate your overall satisfaction with the shopping experience?

I would give it a 9.

Entity: value=”9”
