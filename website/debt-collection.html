Automated Reminders
Virtual voice assistants can automatically call individuals who owe money to remind them of their outstanding debts and discuss payment options. It can help increase the chances of successful collection by ensuring that individuals are reminded of their debts promptly and consistently without requiring manual follow-up efforts.

Hello, this is a payment reminder from XYZ Company. Your payment of $50 is due tomorrow. Please make sure to complete the payment by the due date to avoid any late fees.

Yes, I have not made the payment yet. How can I make it?

Intent: /available_payment_methods

 You can pay through our website or mobile app. I can also help you make a payment right now. What payment option would you like to use?

Outbound Calls
Virtual voice assistants can be used for outbound calls in debt collection by automating the process of contacting individuals who owe money and discussing payment options with them. This can be done by programming the virtual voice assistant with a script that includes information about the debt, payment options, and next steps. 

 Hello, may I please speak with John?

Yes, I am aware. I am unable to pay the full amount at the moment.

Intent: /financial_issues
Automated Payments
Automated payments in debt collection involve using a virtual voice assistant to facilitate payments for outstanding debts. This can be done by integrating the virtual voice assistant with various payment methods such as credit cards, bank transfers,s or other digital wallets, allowing customers to make payments easily and quickly, increasing the chances of successful collection.

I need to pay my outstanding debt.

Intent: /pay_debt
Action - API Call:   check_debt(‘current_phone_number) - status OK;

Thank you! Your outstanding balance is $500. Would you like to make a payment now?

Yes, I want to pay it off.

Intent: /make_payment
Entity: amount=”500 USD”

Great! I can process your payment of $500 now. Please confirm the payment method you would like to use.

Improved Data Management
Virtual voice assistants can help to improve data management by automatically collecting and storing information about customer interactions and making it available for analysis and reporting, which can help to identify patterns, trends, and opportunities to improve the collection process.

Hello, thank you for calling XYZ Company. To help us locate your account and get started, please provide your full name and phone number associated with your account.

Yes, my name is John Smith, and my phone number is (555) 123-4567.

Entity: name=”John Smith”, phone_number=”5551234567”

Thank you, John. Please also provide your account number or social security number.

Sure, my account number is 123456, or my social security number is 123-45-6789.

Increase portfolio management efficiency

The use of virtual voice assistants can help to improve recovery and collection efforts and ultimately lead to taking up more portfolios or covering more customers with the same set of human agents. It can help collection agencies take on more debt portfolios without hiring additional employees.

Reduce collection costs

Virtual voice assistants for debt collection can help lower costs and increase the collection speed by automating many processes. This can include making automated follow-up calls, processing payments, and managing data, which can reduce the need for additional staff and improve the efficiency of the debt collection process.
