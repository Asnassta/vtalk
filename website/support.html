Routing Calls Based
on Users' Intentions 
AI voice assistants can ascertain the caller's intent and transfer them to the appropriate service. vTalk’s AI assistants can also gather pertinent information during a conversation, such as bank details and the goods or services they're inquiring about, and forward it to the agent so they can better serve the customer and end the call faster. 

Hello, I have a question about my account.

Intent: /account_information

Of course, I'll be happy to help. Please provide me with your account number.

Sure, it's 123456.

Entity:  account_number=“123456”
Action - API Call:  check_account_info(“123456”) - status OK;

Thank you. I see you are a VIP client and have your personal account manager. Please hold while I transfer you to your manager.

Troubleshooting
It's common for agents to spend a considerable amount of time on a single problem. Depending on the nature of the problem, vTalk’s AI voice assistant for customer service can troubleshoot independently or with human assistance. If the voice assistant cannot help the consumer, it will transfer the conversation to a human agent and provide the information gathered thus far.

Hi, I'm having trouble with my account. I can't log in. When I try to log in, it says that my password is incorrect. But I know it's the correct password.

Intent: /password_issues

I see. Have you tried resetting your password?

No, I haven't. How do I do that?

Intent: /deny

You can reset your password by clicking on the 'forgot password' link on the login page. It will then prompt you to enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions on resetting your password.

Streamline Support
with AI FAQ Assistance
Virtual voice assistants can be programmed to answer frequently asked questions and provide customers with basic information, helping reduce wait times and improve the overall customer experience. They can also be integrated into existing customer service systems to provide a seamless, efficient support experience.

Hello. Does the bank have online banking and a mobile app?

Intent: /services_information
Entity:  service=[“online banking”, “mobile app”]

Hello. The bank offers customers online banking and a mobile app. You can download the app from the App Store or Google Play to access your accounts, make payments, transfer money, and more. To enroll in online banking, you can visit the bank's website and follow the instructions to sign up for an account.

Secure and Convenient Authentication with Voice AI
vTalk’s AI assistant can be used by voice assistants to identify and verify users through speech safely. The average time spent by a contact center worker on client authentication is a minute; therefore, replacing this manual procedure with an AI voice assistant can result in significant cost savings.

 Hi, I'd like to access my bank account please.

Intent: /identification_request

Of course! To access your account, I'll need to verify your identity. Please provide your account number.

Yes, my account number is 123456789.

Entity: account_number=”123456789”

Thank you for confirming your identity, Mr.Doe. You now have access to your account. How can I assist you today?
