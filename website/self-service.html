Problem resolution
Problem resolution is a key aspect of customer service, and it involves addressing various customer issues, such as returns, cancellations, and complaints. Virtual voice assistants can play a crucial role in streamlining the process of resolving these issues by providing customers with prompt and accurate information and guiding them through the necessary steps to resolve their problems. 

Hi, I'm having an issue with my order. I received the wrong product. I ordered a red shirt but received a blue one.

Intent: /order_issues
Entity: cause=”wrong product”, details=”ordered a red shirt but received a blue one”

I'm sorry to hear that. Please give me your order number so I can look up the details of your purchase.

Sure, it's 123456.

Entity: order_number=”123456”
Action - API Call: reclamation(“123456”,”wrong product”,”ordered a red shirt but received a blue one”) - status OK;

I apologize for the mistake. We can exchange your order. Would you like me to process the exchange now?

Appointments and scheduling
AI-based voice assistants can provide customers with a convenient and efficient way to schedule appointments with a business, such as scheduling a repair service, booking a rental property, or scheduling a doctor's appointment. The AI assistant can understand the customer's needs and preferences and provide options and appointment times that fit their schedule.

I would like to reschedule it for the same time next Friday.
