Automated Customers’ Payments
Automated customers’ payments can be made using virtual voice assistants by integrating them with different payment methods such as credit cards, bank transfers, or digital wallets. This allows customers to make payments over the phone by providing their payment information to the virtual voice assistant, which will process the payment and confirm the transaction. 

Hi, I would like to make a payment on my account number 123456.

Intent: /make_payment
Entity: account_number=”123456”
Action - API Call: check_account(“current_phone_number”, ”123456”) - status OK;

 Thank you. And how much would you like to pay today?

 I'd like to pay $100.

Entity: amount=”100 USD”
Action: Go to /get_payment_source
Balance Inquiries
Balance inquiries allow customers to check their account balance over the phone using a virtual voice assistant. This can be done by integrating the virtual voice assistant with a customer's account information and programming it to provide accurate and up-to-date balance information.

Can you check my account balance, please?

Intent: /check_balance

Hello. To verify your identity, can you please provide your date of birth?

My date of birth is January 1st, 1990.

Entity: birth_date=”01.01.1990”
Action - API Call:  check_balance(“123456”, ”01.01.1990”) - status OK;

Thank you. Your account balance as of today is $500. Is there anything else I can help you with?

Automated
payment reminders
Virtual voice assistants can help remind customers of upcoming or overdue payments. This can be done by programming the virtual voice assistant with a script that includes information about the payment and the due date. This option can increase the chances of successful payment collection.

Hello, this is a payment reminder from XYZ Company. Your payment of $50 is due tomorrow. Please make sure to complete the payment by the due date to avoid any late fees.

Yes, I have not made the payment yet. How can I make it?

Intent: /available_payment_methods

 You can pay through our website or mobile app. I can also help you make a payment right now. What payment option would you like to use?

To check the payment
status 24X7
vTalk.ai virtual voice assistant can be integrated with the payment system, allowing customers to check the status of their payments at any time of the day. It can be done by providing the virtual voice assistant with an order number or other payment information, which it can use to access the relevant payment data and provide an updated status to the customer.

Hi, I wanted to check the status of my last payment. I made a payment a few days ago, and I haven't received any confirmation yet.

Intent: /check_payment_status
Entity: payment_date=”last”

Thank you for reaching out. Please provide me with your account number so I can look at your payment history.

Sure, my account number is 123456789.

Entity: account_number=”123456789”
Action - API Call: check_account(“current_phone_number”, ”123456789”,”last”) - status OK;
Easy and quick refunds
vTalk.ai voice bots can simplify the refund process, increasing customer satisfaction, and loyalty. To process a refund, a virtual voice assistant can be integrated with the payment system and programmed to verify customer information, initiate the refund and provide the customer with confirmation of the transaction.

Hi, I would like to request a refund for my recent purchase.

Intent: /refund_request

I'd be happy to assist you with that. Please provide me with your order number and the reason for the refund.

Sure, my order number is 12345, and I need a refund because the product I received was damaged.

Entity: order_number=”12345”, refund_reason=”damaged product”
Action - API Call: create_refund (”12345”, ”damaged product”) - status OK;

Conversation data analysis uses data from customer interactions to gain insights into customer needs, preferences, and behaviors. It can improve customer service, optimize sales strategies, and drive innovation. Additionally, conversation data analysis can be used to improve the performance of virtual voice assistants by identifying areas for improvement in customer interactions and training the model accordingly.
