Automate your outbound sales calls
Virtual voice assistants can automatically call individuals who owe money to remind them of their outstanding debts and discuss payment options. It can help increase the chances of successful collection by ensuring that individuals are reminded of their debts promptly and consistently without requiring manual follow-up efforts.

Hello, this is John from XYZ Company. I am calling to introduce our new insurance service. Is this Sarah?

Yes, it’s me.

Intent: /affirm

Nice to meet you, Sarah. Our new service includes protection for your home's structure and personal property and liability coverage in case someone is injured on your property. Can I ask you a few questions to determine if our service could fit your needs?

Reminders automation
The virtual voice assistant can send customers information about upcoming appointments, payments, or other important events. A virtual voice assistant can be programmed with a script that includes information about the reminder and the date and time it should be sent. It can provide customers with a convenient and consistent way to stay on top of their schedules and essential tasks.

Hello, this is a payment reminder from XYZ Company. Your payment of $50 is due tomorrow. Please make sure to complete the payment by the due date to avoid any late fees.

Yes, I have not made the payment yet. How can I make it?

Intent: /payment_methods

You can pay through our website or mobile app. I can also help you make a payment right now. What payment option would you like to use?

Conduct surveys
Virtual voice assistants can conduct surveys by calling customers and asking them pre-determined questions. The responses can be recorded and analyzed, providing valuable insights into customer opinions, preferences, and behaviors. Surveys can be used to improve products and services, identify areas for improvement, and measure customer satisfaction.

Hello, John, This is Mark from XYZ Company. Today you purchased a mobile phone on our website. We would appreciate your feedback to help us improve. Please answer a few quick questions.

Okay, sure.

Intent: /affirm

On a scale of 1 to 5, how satisfied are you with your experience with our company?

4.

Entity: value=”4”
Action: Go to /good_level/extended_feedback

Efficient and Effective

With vTalk voice automated system, outbound sales are improved by getting work done on time without wasting human resources and labor. AI outbound calls are done competently while reaching the right number of prospects. It gives time for live agents to complete more complex work. This leaves both Voice AI and the operator to achieve the goal of call center boosting AI outbound sales.

Saves cost

AI outgoing call is cost-effective by reducing expenses like lower cost per acquisition, a charge per unit call, overhead charges, and contracts with call centers. AI for outbound voice calls for sales is so efficient that it requires fewer live operators, making it more cost-effective. With this, the company's revenue increases.

Make calls at the right time

The virtual voice assistant can make calls at the right time by scheduling or using algorithms that determine the best time to reach a customer based on factors such as location, time zone, and past engagement patterns. This can be done by integrating the virtual voice assistant with a CRM system or other data source that provides information on customer availability and preferences.
