Healthcare

Appointments scheduling
vTalk.ai voice assistants allow patients to schedule appointments, provide reminders for upcoming appointments and reschedule or cancel appointments as needed. It can also help patients navigate the process of booking an appointment, answering any questions they might have, and verifying their insurance coverage.

Hello! Can I schedule an appointment with Dr. Smith?

Intent: /schedule_appointment
Entity: doctor=”Smith”

Sure, what date and time suits you the best?

Thursday at 3 PM would be great.

Entity: appointment_date=”Thu, 12-OCT-2021”, slot_time=”15:00”
Action - API Call: create_appointment(“Smith”, ”Thu, 12-OCT-2021”, ”15:00”) - status OK

I see that Dr. Smith is available at this time. So I booked an appointment for you on Thursday, the 12th of October at 3 PM.

Symptom checker
A symptom checker is a tool that allows patients to input their symptoms and receive a list of possible conditions or illnesses that may be causing them. It can assist with self-diagnosis and triage and provide information about the next steps, such as seeking medical care or self-treatment options.

 Hello, John. Could you please describe your symptoms?

Yes, I have a sudden high temperature, an aching body, and a dry cough

Intent: /disease
Entity: symptoms=”high temperature”, cough_type=”dry cough”
Action: Go to /flue

Based on the symptoms you describe, you most likely have the flu. I will now reconnect you to a relevant specialist to discuss the further steps. Please stay online, and wish you happy health, John.

 Thanks!

Action: call_transfer(“flue”)
Prescriptions management
An AI-based virtual voice assistant can assist with managing prescriptions in healthcare by allowing patients to refill and renew their prescriptions, tracking delivery, and providing reminders for medication. It can also assist patients with finding pharmacies, checking prescription drug prices, and providing information about their medications' potential interactions and side effects.

Hi, I'd like to check on the status of my prescription.

Intent: /check_status

Of course! Please provide me with your name and date of birth for verification purposes.

Sure, my name is John Doe and my date of birth is 01/01/1980.

Entity: name=”Jonh Doe”, birth_date=”01.01.1980”
Action - API Call: check_prescription(“John Doe”, ”01.01.1980”) - status OK

Thank you, Mr. Doe. I have found your prescription. It was filled on the 5th of this month and should be ready for pickup at your preferred pharmacy. Is there anything else I can assist you with today?

Claims management
An AI-based virtual voice assistant can assist with healthcare claim management by automating the process of submitting claims, tracking claims status, and providing patients with updates on their claims and payments. It can also assist with verifying coverage, collecting required documentation, and guiding the next steps for denied claims.

Hi, I need to file a claim for my recent hospital visit.

Intent: /claim
Action - API Call:check_claim(“current_phone_number”) - status OK

 I see that your claim has been received and is currently being processed. It may take up to 7 business days for the claim to be fully reviewed and a decision made.

Ok, thanks for the update. Can you send me a confirmation of the claim filing to my email address?

IntenIntent: /confirmation_requestt: /claim
Entity: channel=”email”

 Absolutely, I have sent a confirmation email to your registered email address. If you have further questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out.
